# 🦷 Dental Caries & Demineralization Study

Welcome to this repository focused on the study of **dental caries**, **enamel demineralization**, and oral health education.  
This project was created as an academic and educational material to explain how the caries process begins, develops, and impacts dental structure over time.

---

## 📚 About the Project

This repository contains:

- 📄 PDF study material  
- 🎤 Presentation slides  
- 🔬 Educational content about enamel demineralization  
- 🦷 Explanation of the early stages of dental caries  
- 💡 Preventive oral health concepts  

The goal of this project is to make the understanding of caries formation simpler, more visual, and accessible for students and professionals interested in dentistry.

---

## 🧪 Topics Covered

- Tooth enamel structure  
- Demineralization process  
- Bacterial action and dental biofilm  
- Initial carious lesions  
- Oral pH changes  
- Prevention and remineralization  

---

## 🚀 Objective

This study project aims to improve knowledge about oral health and demonstrate, through educational material and presentation, how dental caries develops from its initial stages.

---

## 📊 Caries Development Process

| Stage | Description | Impact |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| 🟢 Healthy Enamel | Balanced oral environment | Strong enamel |
| 🟡 Initial Demineralization | Mineral loss caused by acids | White spot lesions |
| 🟠 Early Caries | Beginning of enamel breakdown | Increased sensitivity |
| 🔴 Advanced Caries | Deep bacterial progression | Cavities and pain |

---

## 📈 Demineralization Cycle

```text
Sugar Consumption 🍭
        ↓
Bacterial Fermentation 🦠
        ↓
Acid Production ⚠️
        ↓
pH Reduction 📉
        ↓
Enamel Demineralization 🦷
        ↓
Initial Carious Lesion
```
